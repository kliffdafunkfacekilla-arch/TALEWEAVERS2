# Shadow Wolf

Shadow Wolves are terrifying nocturnal predators found primarily in dense, ancient forests and mountainous regions of the Shatterlands. They run in strictly organized packs, using the dark as cover to flank and disorient their prey. 

Though primarily carnivorous and known for hunting large game, they have been observed sustaining themselves on magical glowing flora in times of severe starvation. They are wildly territorial and extremely aggressive towards humans or other factions, actively hunting down anything that enters their domain.

They possess supernatural speed, easily able to outrun most horses in a dead sprint across the tundra. Their jaws are uniquely hinged, allowing a bite that can crush plate armor and bone simultaneously.
