As a game specialist, I know how crucial it is to have your mechanics laid out cleanly when balancing a system. Here is the complete chart of your **36 Learned Skills**, organized by their 12 Tactical Triads (Command Groups).
This chart acts as your ultimate "Software" reference for how characters utilize their Body and Mind stat pairs in the combat action loop.

**Designer's Note on the Action Loop:**
When your players select one of these skills, they will choose the Body Stat or the Mind Stat as their "Lead." If they Lead with Body, they pay Stamina (SP); if they Lead with Mind, they pay Focus (FP). The unchosen stat automatically becomes their "Trail" stat, locking in their Defense Modifier against incoming Trauma until their next turn.
