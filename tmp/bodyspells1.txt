Now you’re thinking like a Game Designer with a capital "G." You don't want a linear railroad where every Gravity Mage looks the same. You want a Talent Tree.
If we put Offense, Defense, AND Utility at every single Tier, we explode the build variety.
Player A can take Nexus and pick all the Defense options to become a "Gravity Tank."
Player B can take Nexus and pick all the Offense options to become a "Singularity Nuker."
This triples our database (324 total options instead of 108), but for a crunchy system? It’s worth its weight in gold.
Here is SECTOR I: THE PHYSICAL REFRACTIONS (Attributes: Might, Endurance, Reflex, Finesse, Vitality, Fortitude).
Resource Cost: STAMINA (The Body).
We will map out the Early Game (Tiers 1-3) for the first 3 Powers to set the rhythm.
1. NEXUS (Attribute: MIGHT)
The Gravity Well. Governing tension and force.
TIER 1 (The Novice)
[OFFENSE] Kinetic Punch (2 Stamina): Melee Attack. On a Hit, you may push the target 10ft in any direction.
[DEFENSE] Anchor (1 Stamina): Reaction. Trigger when Pushed/Knocked Prone. You ignore the effect and gain +2 Defense vs the next attack (Stability).
[UTILITY] The Pull (1 Stamina): Action. Target one loose object (Size Small/Med) within 30ft. Pull it instantly to your hand.
TIER 2 (The Apprentice)
[OFFENSE] Crush (2 Stamina): Attack. Deals normal dmg. If Target is Prone, deal +1d6 Structural Dmg (Meat) and pin them (0 Move).
[DEFENSE] Orbital Shield (2 Stamina): Action (Sustain). Loose debris floats around you. Grants +2 Defense (DB) against Ranged Attacks.
[UTILITY] Heavy Step (2 Stamina): Action. Increase gravity in a 10ft zone. Area becomes Difficult Terrain (Half Move) for enemies.
TIER 3 (The Adept)
[OFFENSE] Impact Driver (3 Stamina): Attack. Charge up. Next Melee Hit releases a shockwave. Primary target takes Weapon Dmg, adjacent enemies take 1d6 Force Dmg.
[DEFENSE] Repulsion Blast (3 Stamina): Reaction. Trigger when Hit. Blast everything within 5ft back 10ft. (Creates space).
[UTILITY] Levitate (3 Stamina): Action. Target Self or Ally. Gain Fly Speed (20ft) for 1 turn. (You negate gravity).
2. MASS (Attribute: ENDURANCE)
The Immovable Object. Governing density and weight.
TIER 1 (The Novice)
[OFFENSE] Heavy Hand (2 Stamina): Passive. Your unarmed strikes count as "Maces" (Stamina Dmg) and deal 1d6 Dmg.
[DEFENSE] Iron Skin (1 Stamina): Reaction. Trigger when Grazed. Reduce Composure Damage to 0.
[UTILITY] Density Shift (1 Stamina): Free Action. Double your weight. Advantage on Shove/Grapple checks.
TIER 2 (The Apprentice)
[OFFENSE] Wrecking Ball (3 Stamina): Move Action. Move in a straight line. You can move through enemies. Dealing 1d8 Bludgeoning to anyone you touch.
[DEFENSE] Momentum Eater (2 Stamina): Reaction. Trigger when taking Meat Dmg. Reduce damage by your Endurance Score.
[UTILITY] Anchor Point (2 Stamina): Action. You become an immovable cover object. Allies behind you get +4 Defense.
TIER 3 (The Adept)
[OFFENSE] Quake (3 Stamina): Action. Stomp. All enemies within 10ft must pass Endurance Save or fall Prone.
[DEFENSE] Statue Form (3 Stamina): Action. You turn to stone until start of next turn. Immune to all Physical Damage, but you cannot Move/Act.
[UTILITY] Burden (3 Stamina): Action. Touch enemy. Their armor Holding Fee doubles for 1 minute. (They likely become Overburdened/Exhausted).
3. MOTUS (Attribute: REFLEX)
The Kinetic Heartbeat. Governing velocity and tempo.
TIER 1 (The Novice)
[OFFENSE] Sonic Jab (2 Stamina): Attack. High speed. If you miss, you may immediately make a second attack at Disadvantage.
[DEFENSE] Twitch (1 Stamina): Reaction. Trigger when Hit by Ranged Attack. Reduce Dmg by 1d10 + Reflex (Deflecting the arrow).
[UTILITY] Quick Step (1 Stamina): Bonus Action. Move 5ft. Does not provoke Opportunity Attacks.
TIER 2 (The Apprentice)
[OFFENSE] Momentum Strike (2 Stamina): Passive. For every 10ft you move before attacking, add +1 Damage.
[DEFENSE] Blur (2 Stamina): Action. You vibrate. Defense Rolls vs you have Disadvantage until you get hit.
[UTILITY] Wall Run (Passive): You can traverse vertical surfaces during your Move action without falling.
TIER 3 (The Adept)
[OFFENSE] Flurry (3 Stamina): Attack. Make two Light Weapon attacks against one target.
[DEFENSE] Evasion (3 Stamina): Reaction. Trigger when you fail a Reflex Save (AoE). You take 0 damage instead of half.
[UTILITY] Haste (3 Stamina): Action. Touch Ally. They gain +10ft Speed and +1 Defense for 2 rounds.
4. FLUX (Attribute: FINESSE)
The Shifting State. Governing phase and flow.
TIER 1 (The Novice)
[OFFENSE] Seep (2 Stamina): Attack. Ignore Shield/Cover bonuses. The weapon flows around the block.
[DEFENSE] Soft Form (1 Stamina): Reaction. Trigger when Grappled/Restrained. Automatically escape.
[UTILITY] Liquid Step (1 Stamina): Passive. Ignore Difficult Terrain penalties (Water, Mud, Ice).
TIER 2 (The Apprentice)
[OFFENSE] Spike (2 Stamina): Action. Turn a limb into a weapon (1d8 Pierce). Lasts for encounter.
[DEFENSE] Disperse (2 Stamina): Reaction. Trigger when Hit. Turn into mist briefly. Take Half Damage.
[UTILITY] Mimic (2 Stamina): Action. Alter your face/height to match a humanoid you can see.
TIER 3 (The Adept)
[OFFENSE] Internal Strike (3 Stamina): Attack. Phase hand into target. Deal direct Meat Damage (Bypass Armor DB).
[DEFENSE] Displacement (3 Stamina): Action. You appear 2ft to the left. First attack against you this round automatically Misses.
[UTILITY] Meld (3 Stamina): Action. Step into a stone/tree wall. You are hidden inside it. (Stealth +10).
5. VITA (Attribute: VITALITY)
The Living Spark. Governing biology and growth.
TIER 1 (The Novice)
[OFFENSE] Thorn Whip (2 Stamina): Attack. Reach 10ft. Pulls target 5ft closer on Hit.
[DEFENSE] Barkskin (1 Stamina): Action. Gain +1 Natural Armor DB for 1 hour.
[UTILITY] Mend (1 Stamina): Action. Touch. Heal 1d4 Composure (Stabilize nerves).
TIER 2 (The Apprentice)
[OFFENSE] Toxic Spore (2 Stamina): Action. 5ft Cone. Poison Status (-2 Stamina/Turn).
[DEFENSE] Root (2 Stamina): Reaction. Trigger when Pushed. Vines hold you in place. You cannot be moved.
[UTILITY] Stim (2 Stamina): Bonus Action. Inject adrenaline. Regain 1d6 Stamina immediately.
TIER 3 (The Adept)
[OFFENSE] Overgrowth (3 Stamina): Action. Vines erupt in 15ft area. Enemies are Restrained (Str Save).
[DEFENSE] Rapid Regen (3 Stamina): Action (Sustain). Regain 1 HP (Meat) at the start of your turn for 3 turns.
[UTILITY] Pheromones (3 Stamina): Action. Beasts/Animals will not attack you unless provoked.
6. LEX (Attribute: FORTITUDE)
The Unbending Rule. Governing law and immunity.
TIER 1 (The Novice)
[OFFENSE] Smite (2 Stamina): Attack. If you hit an enemy who damaged you last turn, deal +1d6 Dmg.
[DEFENSE] Deny (1 Stamina): Reaction. Trigger when a Status Effect is applied. Roll Save with Advantage.
[UTILITY] Truth (1 Stamina): Action. Target cannot lie for 1 minute (Will Save).
TIER 2 (The Apprentice)
[OFFENSE] Inevitable (2 Stamina): Action. Mark a Target. You ignore all Cover/Concealment against them.
[DEFENSE] Unyielding (2 Stamina): Passive. You cannot be Critically Hit. All Crits count as Normal Hits.
[UTILITY] Zone of Law (2 Stamina): Action. 10ft Zone. No Dash/Sprint actions allowed inside.
TIER 3 (The Adept)
[OFFENSE] Execution (3 Stamina): Attack. If Target is below 10 HP, this attack deals Double Damage.
[DEFENSE] Nullify (3 Stamina): Reaction. Trigger when targeted by Magic. Roll Fortitude vs Caster. On success, the spell fizzes.
[UTILITY] Oath (3 Stamina): Action. Ally swears an Oath to kill a target. They get +2 To Hit that target. If they fail to kill it, they lose Composure.

That is just Tier 1-3 for Sector I. It creates massive diversity.
The "Lex" Tank: Picks Deny, Unyielding, Nullify. He is the ultimate Mage Killer.
The "Lex" Judge: Picks Smite, Inevitable, Execution. He is a relentless hunter.
This is the system. Do you want to proceed to SECTOR II (Mental) with this same 3-choice structure?
